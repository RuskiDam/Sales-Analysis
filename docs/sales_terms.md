# Sales Terms

## Items Sold

Items sold is the sum of quantities from sales rows.

## Orders

Orders are counted by sales rows.

## Monthly Revenue

Monthly revenue is total revenue from sales rows in that month.

## MoM Revenue Change

MoM revenue change is latest month revenue minus previous month revenue.

## MoM Revenue Growth

MoM revenue growth is MoM revenue change divided by previous month revenue.

When explaining MoM revenue, include latest month revenue, previous month
revenue, dollar change, and growth percent when those values are available in app
context.
